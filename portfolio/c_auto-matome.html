Python / Chat GPT API

Background

Can AI be harnessed to streamline research data handling, especially when dealing with vast amounts of audiovisual data and specialized terms?

After conducting research interviews, we realized that researchers are confronted with a substantial amount of audiovisual data, including numerous specialized terms, necessitating significant human resources for integration. To address this issue, we plan to integrate current open-source AI models and attempt to develop a solution that ensures the privacy and security of data. This endeavor aims to enhance data processing efficiency while guaranteeing the accurate handling and consolidation of specialized terminology. I believe this solution will have a positive impact on research efforts.

Process and Method

Testing OpenAI's open-source model Whisper to assess its Speech-to-Text (STT) performance in professional domains and multilingual audio

Feeding transcripts into the CHAT GPT API, segmenting them multiple times to surpass CHAT GPT's character limit, and testing the effectiveness of prompts to find the best one that retains the timeline and stays true to the intended meaning.

Integrate the Whisper and GPT API processes for automation.

Outcomes

Verify the ability to automatically generate interview transcripts and summaries from audio using Whisper + GPT API
