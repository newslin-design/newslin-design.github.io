Background

The high cost and limited ability of on-site demonstration in the Pro AV market is a challenge that can affect customer purchasing decisions.

When selling products in the Pro audio/visual (AV) market, demonstrating a comprehensive spatial solution with diverse product combinations is crucial as it directly impacts the customer's willingness to place an order.

However, the on-site DEMO ROOM is costly and cannot simultaneously showcase various product combinations, and inviting customers to visit is challenging. This project aims to develop a virtual DEMO ROOM that users can experience online. Additionally, it will create a function library that rapidly adds various solutions to the online DEMO ROOM in response to market changes.
