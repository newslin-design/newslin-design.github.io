2021

In light of the profound changes in meeting and education scenarios resulting from the 2020 epidemic, we collaborated with our R&D teams to develop this software. We closely considered feedback from actual customers in the field to precisely define user requirements and software specifications.

Our software offers a unique solution by creating a virtual USB channel, securely receiving one or multiple remote video streams from hardware devices. This enables system integrators to swiftly deploy a remote meeting or education environment, catering to the evolving needs of our customers.
