This project entails three key objectives: swiftly identifying the market fit for the new AI-based algorithm, developing a solution that caters to user needs while leveraging available R&D resources, and formulating a comprehensive business strategy.

The proposed solution is a Text-to-Speech (TTS) service that harnesses cutting-edge deep learning technology. It enables effortless integration into any speech-enabled application or device, delivering lifelike Mandarin Chinese voice synthesis that closely resembles native speech, requiring minimal effort.

Build minimum viable products to find the market needs

The first problem with this project is that the development time is too slow to support this type of project, and at the end of the project must roll out a complete application. But we need a simple prototype to explore the market and verify whether our core functions are in need. To solve this problem, I through the following adjustment to accelerate the development.

1. Define the market we want to explore, detailed specifications and priorities:

Make choosing or sorting the features more logical in the roadmap or PRD based on the market.
In terms of market segmentation, through sales feedback, we define two target markets to test the prototype:

1. a small business ( like YouTubers)
2. corporation staff ( like News reporters).

And we use different sales strategies and create two applications that can meet different scenarios and are more accessible to make function trade-offs. Then Small business is through marketing promotion. Corporation through sales and BD promotion.

2. Adjust the specifications flexibly with the restriction and reduce the QA cost and schedule

Build a fast communication workflow, more closely cooperate with the RD team, and adjust the specifications, framework, interaction, and other details with difficulty or restriction.
Re-define which function would be needed to verify, such as how many phone models or browsers could be supported.

Through the above adjustment, the project's NRE (Non-Recurring Engineering) can be significantly reduced, and the scale of the project will be smaller. This can give PMs more space to open the next project for this solution.

Based on feedback, put forward a Service Proposal

Based on the market and sales feedback, we found some potential market demands, and we put forward design proposals for this:

Proposal 1, Translating with your voice: Use the speaker's voice to shorten the distance between the audience in a multilingual situation.

Many corporations will use interpreters to dub in international remote conferences/seminars, which are becoming more and more during the lockdown. But this often leads to feeling apart from the audience. In some situations, they need to use their voice to shorten the distance from the audience.

Proposal 2, Fast voice generation service with a large number of voice actors: Provide a secure and dedicated rental, private cloud server with multiple voice actors

With the vigorous development of podcasts, the sound economy is on the rise. Many news, magazines, story books, and other manuscripts in the market need to be converted into speech by a different voice.

Definition

Based on the technical feasibility and channel strategy, PM selects Proposal 2 as the development direction.
