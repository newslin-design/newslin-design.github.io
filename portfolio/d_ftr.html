Future Training Room Solution design
Multiple o devices control scenario designs

As digitization continues to advance, corporate training has evolved into a more diverse landscape, necessitating the development of new training room solutions to meet market demands. The objective of this project is to identify a solution that addresses the requirements of teachers in digital settings, serving as a compelling success story for international sales.

Task

# Discover the trend in education, and define the scenario with the project manager and HR team
# Propose the solutions, and build the detail about them.
# Complete the control system UI with FAE teams

Background

With the accelerating digitization, teachers need new training room solutions.

With the advancement of technology and accelerating digitization, teaching and knowledge transmission methods are becoming more and more diverse. The teaching site has gradually changed from traditional one-way knowledge content transmission to multiple ideological interactions, such as workshops, group discussions, Remote cooking classes, and Remote field education.

With more and more interactions in the classroom, the dependence on many digital devices and software will become deeper and deeper, which is a new challenge for teachers and classroom managers.
With this project, the company not only meets the needs of teachers but also hopes to become an indicator of future classroom solutions launched by the company.

And we aim to achieve fast and high-quality connection through our products to digitize interaction without threshold. Through the control system, we can freely allocate and control all equipment to reduce the difficulty of learning how to use it. Eliminate the high wall between equipment, teachers, and students, and combine more third-party applications to achieve a borderless classroom that can interact across every space.

Define requirements

Through interviews with classroom teachers, we define various situations and user journeys.
And with workshops, we explore new ways of application that meet the needs of users.

Hardware Equipment Planning

Discuss with system planners to develop a hardware solution that meets the requirements.

Based on the requirements,
we have planned two solutions that can be switched at any time

Lecture-based teaching (including remote mode)

Lecture-based teaching can meet the traditional teaching style of most lecturers. In addition to perfect information presentation, many new communication methods are added, such as video recording, timely voting, questioning system, etc. This lowers the communication threshold between teachers and students, facilitates the unification of student opinions, increases the willingness of remote students to interact, and allows more communication and knowledge collision in the traditional classroom style.

Collaborative teaching (including remote mode)

Collaborative teaching offers a variety of collaborative work, including small group discussions, workshops, Open Space Technology, remote collaboration, and more.

Control system design

Use 3D images to show the scene, let teachers can quickly understand

Using 3D images to show up to 15 different screens and 21 signal sources in the classroom, multiple device operations are integrated into specific contexts for teachers to use quickly.
